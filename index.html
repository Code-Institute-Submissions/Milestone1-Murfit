My Journey

Who am i?

Hi, I'm Caitriona! I am also known as Cait so feel free to call me that as well! I am a fun loving girl who loves to work hard and play hard! I aim to get moving everyday as it gives me so much energy!

I have strong beliefs in having a balanced lifestyle. Yes it's important to get moving, but we all need to have fun and remember why we do it! Life is here for living, but hey, lets feel good about ourselves when we are strutting our stuff on the dancefloor or doing the groceries in the shop!

My Journey

My journey to where I am now is nothing magical or special, juat one of giving everything a chance! I attended my first circuit class with my mother at 15 and I never looked back! I always knew I loved running and moving and I continue to do this everyday.

The more fitness classes I attended the more i fell in love, so much so I wanted to help others on their journey. If it wasn't for my mother bringing me along I may never have went, so I am here to help otehrs take their first step, and give them guidance to a better lifestyle.

Murfit

Why Join?

Murfit is a fun loving way to get moving with the focus on jumping, sqautinf, stretching and lifting. There is no big magic formula to bamboozle you with! These are the simple movement that you should be doing everyday to ensure a happy and healthy lifestyle!

Why Wait?

Time is ticking! This is your chance to train with like-minded women who all have the same goal, to get up, to get moving, to get healthy. At Murfit we are here to get the best out of you and to get you feeling confident and strong, no matter where you start! Dont wait, make that first step or 3rd step, come and join the movement to a better you!
